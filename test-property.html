About the property

Property overview

Discover JW Marriott Scottsdale Camelback Inn Resort & Spa, an award-winning resort near Camelback Mountain in Paradise Valley, AZ. The resort features deluxe amenities, heated outdoor pools, garden terraces, and desert trails through the Sonoran Desert.

Take the complimentary shuttle to the 18-hole championship golf courses or treat yourself to a refreshing massage in the tranquil day spa. Relax in casitas and suites with sophisticated furnishings and outdoor living spaces, many with sweeping mountain views.
